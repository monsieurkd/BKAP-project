Summer Kickoff Party

This event takes place at the start of summer and is the perfect way to kick off the season.

Modal title

Our Summer Kickoff Party is a great way to start the summer season. We have music, food, and activities planned throughout the day to keep the whole family entertained. For sports lovers, we have a beach volleyball tournament, and for those who want to chill out, there are plenty of lounge chairs and cabanas. We also have a barbecue where you can enjoy some delicious food while taking a break from the water activities. And to cap off the day, we have a spectacular fireworks display in the evening that is not to be missed.

Pirate Adventure

Ahoy, matey! Our Pirate Adventure event is a hit with families and kids of all ages.

Modal title

Our Pirate Adventure event is a swashbuckling good time for families and kids of all ages. Come dressed in your best pirate costume and enjoy a day filled with themed activities. We have a treasure hunt where you can search for treasure throughout the park, a water gun fight where you can engage in some friendly competition, and a pirate-themed photo booth where you can take memorable photos with your family and friends. And don't forget to keep an eye out for our resident pirates, who are always up to some mischief.

Halloween Spooktacular

Our Halloween event is not to be missed. We have spooky decorations, a haunted house, and a costume contest with prizes for the best costumes.

Modal title

Our Halloween event is one of the most popular events of the year. We transform the park into a spooky wonderland with decorations, lighting, and a haunted house. Kids can dress up in their favorite costumes and participate in a costume contest with prizes for the best costumes. And for those who are brave enough, we have a haunted maze that is sure to send chills down your spine. We also have a DJ playing some spooky tunes to keep the party going all night long.

Christmas Festival

Our Christmas Festival is a magical event that transforms the park into a winter wonderland.

Modal title

Our Christmas Festival is a magical event that transforms the park into a winter wonderland. We have a giant Christmas tree, holiday-themed light displays, and an ice skating rink where you can glide around under the stars. Kids can also visit Santa in his grotto and have their photo taken with him. And for those who love to shop, we have a Christmas market where you can find unique gifts and holiday treats.

New Year's Eve Bash

Ring in the New Year with us at our New Year's Eve Bash. We have a live DJ, party favors, and a midnight countdown with a fireworks show.

Modal title

Our New Year's Eve Bash is the perfect way to ring in the new year. We have a live DJ playing the latest hits, party favors, and a midnight countdown with a fireworks display that will light up the night sky. There are plenty of food and drink options available throughout the night, so you can keep the energy going until the early hours of the morning.

Luau

Our Hawaiian Luau is a fun and festive event that transports you to a tropical paradise.

Modal title

Our Hawaiian Luau is a fun and festive event that transports you to a tropical paradise. We have hula dancers, a fire knife performance, and a traditional Hawaiian feast with dishes like kalua pork, poke, and poi. You can also learn how to make a lei or try your hand at hula dancing. It's the perfect way to soak up the sun and experience a taste of Hawaii without leaving the park.
